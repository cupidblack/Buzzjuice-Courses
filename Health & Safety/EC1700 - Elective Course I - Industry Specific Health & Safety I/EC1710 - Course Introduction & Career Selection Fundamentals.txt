?? EC1711 - Career Notes (Ghana Context)
* Fast-growing H&S fields: Construction, Energy & Renewables, Transport & Logistics, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Mining, ICT Infrastructure.
* Long-term indicators: demand for NEBOSH, IOSH, NVTI certifications; strong regulation; transferable technical skills.
* Avoid “dead-end” jobs: check automation risks, training support, adaptability across industries.
* Integrity Reminder: falsifying safety/training records damages employability in regulated sectors.

?? EC1714 - Case Study: Warehouse Near-Miss (Tema, 2023)
Scenario: Night-shift near-miss due to pallet stack collapse.
Findings: Poor stacking SOPs, inadequate lighting, untrained casual staff.
Action: Compact HIRA conducted ? SOPs revised, staff briefed, racking inspected.
Outcome: Zero further incidents in six months.
Class task (10 min): List 3 hazards + risk rating + control measures for a similar Ghanaian warehouse. Use ILO video reference for prevention methods.
