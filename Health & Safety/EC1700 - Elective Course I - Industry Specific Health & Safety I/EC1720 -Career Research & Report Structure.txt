?? EC1722 - Career Report — ?2 pages
Page 1 — Career Snapshot (Header Bar in Buzzjuice Blue)
* Role Title & Sector (1 line)
* Key Duties (3–5 bullets)
* Typical Work Settings & Shifts
* Required Qualifications & Typical Local Salary Range
* Core Competencies (Technical & Soft Skills)
Page 2 — Career Pathway & Compact HIRA
* Career pathway: Immediate (0–6 m), Short (6–24 m), Mid (2–5 yrs)
* Training & certifications: essential vs recommended (NEBOSH, First Aid, local accreditations)
