BUS1300 — Affiliate Management (Kowear Affiliate Program)
Course Overview
 BUS1300 is a comprehensive, Kowear-focused affiliate management course designed to equip learners with both strategic and operational skills needed to succeed in Kowear’s AffiliateWP ecosystem. The program blends marketing fundamentals, platform technical fluency, multi-tier commission modeling, legal compliance, and fieldwork to ensure affiliates operate profitably, ethically, and sustainably. Learners explore Tier-1 flat commissions (37%), residual payouts across five tiers, lifetime and recurring referral strategies, and automated membership gating via the jewel_affiliate role. Through hands-on assignments, practical case studies, and scenario simulations, affiliates learn to recruit, mentor, and support referrals, reconcile offline and online campaigns, and scale operations while safeguarding brand integrity, ensuring accurate payouts, and adhering to ‘Know Your Customer' (KYC) and tax obligations. The course emphasizes measurable KPIs, structured weekly schedules, and realistic projections to make part-time and full-time affiliate work both feasible and rewarding.
Gating and Subscription Mechanics
 Lessons 1–4 are free and open to all learners, providing foundational knowledge and basic platform navigation. Lessons 5–8 are gated behind Kowear's jewel_affiliate WordPress user-role, assigned automatically upon successful completion of the Activation Assessment and subscription to a Jewel Affiliate membership subscription plan. The Jewel Affiliate subscription options include monthly (42 GHC), quarterly (77 GHC), semi-annual (127 GHC), and annual (230 GHC), while regular non-affiliate subscriptions remain available but do not grant access to operational secrets or commissions. The course clarifies responsibilities for taxes, referral conduct, and event liabilities, ensuring affiliates understand that only active jewel_affiliate roleholders are eligible for residual commissions and gated content access.


 
 
Lesson 1 — Foundations of Affiliate Marketing & the Koware Affiliate Program (120 minutes)
Lesson Summary
 Learners gain a deep understanding of affiliate marketing principles, ethical and legal boundaries, Koware’s product ecosystem, and commission structures. Practical exercises cover Tier-1 flat commissions (37%), residual distributions through Tier 5, recurring and lifetime revenue models, and baseline micro-schedules suitable for employed or unemployed affiliates. Learners develop a four-week plan projecting realistic earnings, explore starter fieldwork norms, and learn how to strategically subsidize a referral’s first month while remaining compliant.
1.     Topic 1 — Affiliate Marketing Fundamentals, KPIs & Commission Math
 This topic introduces key performance indicators (visits, CTR, conversion rates, LTV, EPC) contextualized using Koware pricing and commissions. Learners practice modeling revenue in spreadsheets, calculate per-sale payouts, simulate residual distributions across Tiers 2–5, and identify break-even points after printing and subscription costs. By producing a short earnings projection, affiliates understand differences between part-time and full-time commitments.
2.     Topic 2 — Koware Product Ecosystem & Buyer Mapping
 Affiliates explore Koware’s offerings—including Buzzjuice Courses, Classies Chronicles, Lifestyle & Talent Grooming, Fashion lines, and third-party products—and learn to map each SKU to buyer personas. The topic covers recurring, lifetime, and one-time products, teaching learners how to prioritize SKUs in outreach while remaining compliant and maximizing lifetime value.
3.     Topic 3 — Multi-Tier Residual Logic & Scenarios
 Learners examine the multi-tier flow: Tier-1 flat 37% commission, with Tier 2 receiving 40% of Tier 1, Tier 3 30%, Tier 4 20%, and Tier 5 10%. Scenarios cover one-time sales, subscription renewals, and lifetime commissions. Learners compute payouts across all tiers, understand timing of payments, and learn how refunds and chargebacks affect downstream commissions.
4.     Topic 4 — Legality, Ethics & Affiliate Disclosures
 This topic distinguishes Koware from illegal pyramid schemes, emphasizing product-centered revenue. Affiliates learn required disclosure language for online/offline promotions, KYC expectations for Ghanaian and international affiliates, and practical steps to avoid misleading claims. Learners draft compliant promotional copy and disclosures for service cards and social posts.
5.     Topic 5 — Practical Onboarding & Starter Kit Checklist
 Learners follow a detailed checklist covering account activation, KYC uploads, Affiliate Portal orientation, UTM/QR templates, 50 printed sample cards, WhatsApp messaging sequences, and manual reconciliation ledgers. Completion and submission of this checklist demonstrate readiness to engage in real campaigns.
6.     Topic 6 — Time Budgets & Sustainable Weekly Schedules
 Learners receive frameworks for 20–120 minutes/day commitments and learn batching strategies, delegation options, and achievable daily outreach targets. The lesson emphasizes work-life balance, avoiding burnout, and integrating affiliate tasks efficiently into daily routines.
7.     Topic 7 — First-Week Break-Even Tactics & Subsidizing Referrals
 This topic explores strategies to accelerate referral break-even, including subsidizing the first month when justified. Learners learn who qualifies for subsidies, documentation protocols, ethical considerations, and tracking methods to protect financial and operational integrity.
8.     Topic 8 — Case Study: Part-Time Affiliate Month 1
 A practical case follows a part-time affiliate who implements time budgets, distributes 150 cards, subsidizes one referral, and reconciles outcomes with AffiliateWP exports. Learners identify attribution gaps, correction strategies, and assess whether subsidy investments yield expected returns.
9.     Topic 9 — Discussion Quiz & Resources
 Learners respond to five questions testing comprehension, such as calculating break-even points, recognizing pyramid red flags, reconciling missing referrals, deciding when to subsidize, and planning weekly time commitments. Supplemental resources include AffiliateWP documentation, UTM/QR tutorials, copywriting guides, and Kowear strategic references.

 Affiliates configure payout methods, upload verification documents according to Koware policy, understand minimum withdrawal thresholds, and accept responsibility for transaction fees. 5.     Topic 5 — Manual Ledger Templates & Reconciliation Workflows

Exercises include reconciling ledger entries against portal exports and creating a dispute packet with timestamps and screenshots for any missing referrals. 6.     Topic 6 — Handling Missing Referrals & Admin Escalation
 This topic presents a structured escalation path: verify URLs/UTMs, check cookie expirations, confirm purchase flow, and prepare evidence for submission. Affiliates learn expected SLAs for admin responses and documentation standards needed to resolve disputes quickly and effectively.
7.     Topic 7 — Platform Troubleshooting & Timing Considerations
 Learners study common platform issues, such as cookie misattribution, delayed referral processing due to payment gateways, and coupon misconfiguration. Exercises include sandbox tests, identifying errors, and coordinating with platform admins to prevent recurring issues while maintaining campaign continuity.
8.     Topic 8 — Case Study: Reconcile a 200-Card Batch
 A hands-on case challenges learners to reconcile 200 offline-distributed cards, including 25 converted subscriptions and 3 disputed attributions. Affiliates prepare dispute packets, collaborate with admins, and compute corrected payouts across multi-tier commissions, documenting all steps for full auditability.
9.     Topic 9 — Discussion Quiz & Resources
 Five scenario-based questions test learners on UTM formatting, dispute evidence, recurring referral credits, escalation procedures, and expired coupon handling. Resources include AffiliateWP docs, UTM guides, QR generation tools, and Koware platform references to strengthen independent problem-solving skills.
