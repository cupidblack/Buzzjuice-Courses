Buzzjuice Courses — Registration & Orientation
Updated 30/03/2026

Welcome to Buzzjuice Courses!
Buzzjuice Courses gives you the learning, digital credentials, and income opportunities you need – all on a single platform designed for education, compliance, and growth.
This short orientation (about 60–75 minutes) will show you how to:
* Set up your profile and registration info
* Understand course structure, subscriptions, and access rules
* Know the requirements for platform use and earning potential
* Get ready for assessments and understand support options
* Receive your first intro to the Buzzjuice Affiliate Program (BUS1300)

What You’ll Do in this Orientation
You’ll complete seven streamlined lessons (each about 5 minutes) and finish with a one-time, 20-minute quiz (20 questions, no passing grade required).
When you pass, you’ll unlock the “Registered & Oriented” badge/certificate and gain full access to your Courses Landing Page for all Buzzjuice programs.

Important: Registration & Identity Confirmation
To progress and unlock future earning opportunities (especially with the affiliate program), you’ll need to complete your profile and verify your identity:
* Ghanaian citizens: Ghana National ID
* Non-Ghanaians outside Ghana: Valid national ID or passport
* Non-Ghanaians living in Ghana: Foreigner’s Ghana Card and valid Ghana work permit
Be sure to have your documents ready when prompted.

Explore More Than Learning: Affiliate Program (Preview)
After orientation, you’ll be able to choose from several course pathways.
Ready to earn? The BUS1300 — Affiliate Management course will introduce you to affiliate marketing via the Koware model:
* Get started free: Foundation lessons are open to all
* Learn practical skills and compliance fundamentals
* When ready to advance and earn, submit your ID/KYC, pass the affiliate onboarding assessment, and activate the Jewel Affiliate Subscription for advanced (gated) content and commission access
Support is available at every point, with clear help channels and community groups.

What Happens After This?
When you complete orientation and pass the quiz, you’ll:
* Earn your digital badge and certificate
* Open your personalized Courses Landing Page
* Choose to start Health & Safety courses or BUS1300 — Affiliate Management and more
* Maintain your subscription to keep access open

Your Success Journey Begins
Follow each lesson, take notes, and complete all steps as guided.
This one-time orientation unlocks your full academic and earning journey on Buzzjuice Courses.

Ready to begin? Let’s get started!

* Your subscription is the key that unlocks Buzzjuice.
* All core features—including the Orientation final quiz, Health & Safety modules, and affiliate (BUS1300) advanced lessons—require an active subscription.
* Subscriptions help maintain fairness, quality, and sustainability of the program.
Key Point: No subscription = no access to assessments, certificates, or advanced content.

1. Active subscription
2. Completed Orientation (“Oriented” status)

* All Health & Safety and advanced affiliate content uses this system (look for the “exclusive-content” tag)

* Lessons 1–4: FREE to all users who pass Orientation

o Complete affiliate activation and KYC

o Provide your order number for the fastest support

* Lose subscription? Just renew to unlock courses/assessments again.

1?? What is BUS1300 — Affiliate Management?
BUS1300 is Buzzjuice’s official pathway to becoming a certified affiliate marketer, built on the proven Koware multi-tier commission model.
Key highlights:
* Free to start: Foundation lessons (1–4) are available immediately after completing orientation.
* Earn as you learn: Earn commissions not just on your referrals, but as your network grows—up to 5 levels deep.

– Unlocks all advanced affiliate lessons (Lessons 5–8), dashboards, commissions, and your full earning potential. Payment options include mobile money, card, or bank transfer.

* Develop marketable skills: Digital marketing, networking, leadership, and platform tools.
